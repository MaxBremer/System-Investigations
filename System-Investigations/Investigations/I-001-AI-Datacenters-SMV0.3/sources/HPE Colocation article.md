## Key Takeaways

- Colocation is leasing space within a larger data center you don't own.
- Typically its BYOH, bring your own hardware.
- Can range in scale; single/multiple racks, cages/cabinets, or rooms.
- Additional services offered as part of rental tend to be:
	- power
	- network connectivity
	- security
- Contracts guarantee a certain amount of uptime
- Contracts also usually specify power sources, backups, etc.
