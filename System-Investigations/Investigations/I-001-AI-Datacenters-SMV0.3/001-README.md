# Investigation 001
## AI Datacenters

**Status**
- Phase: Opening investigations
- Current focus: Building foundation
- Next action: Finish readme

---

# Central Question

Why are tech giants and AI companies pouring large amounts of capital into datacenters with little guarantee of long-term return

---

# Why This Matters

Like it or not, the modern economic landscape is dominated by this kind of spending, with landmark investments from players across the board in datacenters and AI infrastructure. 
Presumably these actors possess some rationale driving them. Understanding this rationale would greatly help my understanding of the modern economic landscape as a whole.
Additionally this investigation is likely to uncover additional interesting topics to explore.

---

# Scope

Included:

- Capital flows of Datacenter construction.
- Participants in Datacenter construction
	- Incentives for these participants.
- Physical inputs/materials for datacenter construction
- Major constraints involved in datacenter construction.

Excluded:

- Achievability of AGI or generalized hypothesizing on the future of AI.
- Literal physical processes of semiconductor fabrication
- Stock price forecasting
- Electrical-grid economics insofar as it does not directly relate to datacenter construction.
